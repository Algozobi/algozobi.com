Algozobi is a school project with focus on AI and Machine Learning projects. The main goal of Algozobi is to represent who we are and our projects both for school purpose and LIA applications as a reference. Zobi is currently the main project that we are working on which is an AI assistant with ability to everyday chat for curing psychological problems. In the future Zobi will be the influencer of our startup and will be able to present our project (real time sign language detection using deep learning) in the coming lecture in school.
